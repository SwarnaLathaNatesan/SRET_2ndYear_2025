* 4-bit binary can only represent numbers up to 15 (1111)

Why This Matters

Understanding binary representation is fundamental to computer science. Each bit position represents a power of 2, with the rightmost bit representing 20 (1), the next 21 (2), then 22 (4), and so on.

This is why powers of 2 are so important in computing - they directly correspond to the bit positions in binary numbers.
